Profile Overview

I’m Hasin Rehana, a PhD researcher in AI and Biomedical NLP at the University of North Dakota. I specialize in applying Large Language Models (LLMs) such as BERT, GPT, and LLaMA to biomedical literature mining tasks, including cancer vaccine discovery, protein-protein interaction extraction, and nested named entity recognition.

My work integrates deep learning, multimodal models, and bioinformatics to solve complex health informatics challenges. I actively contribute to peer-reviewed publications, open-source tools, and international conferences.
