Profile Overview

I’m Hasin Rehana, a PhD researcher in AI and Biomedical NLP at the University of North Dakota. My work lies at the intersection of deep learning, large language models (LLMs), and biomedical informatics — focusing on cancer vaccine discovery, protein-protein interaction mining, and biomedical entity recognition. With hands-on experience in BERT, GPT, and LLaMA models, I build innovative NLP pipelines for real-world medical text mining problems. I contribute to open-source projects, present research at international conferences, and publish in peer-reviewed journals.
